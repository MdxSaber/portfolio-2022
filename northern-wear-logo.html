Northern Waer Logo -

The Design and Final logo

1. What does your brand stand for?

My brand stands for Quality, Customer Service and Teamwork. I created this brand by keeping these three standards in mind. The Quality of the work matters for a business to grow, Customer Service is a must factor to look when opening a business to get more people to buy your products and Teamwork of the employees is a factor that really affect the well being of a business.

2. How does your logo represent your brand? What does it communicate and how do your design decisions help communicate your message?

To break it simple while making this logo I kept two things in mind first being a cafe it has to include something related to the cafe items like coffee, kettle or cookie and after seeing most of the cafe or coffee brand logos I decided to use the coffee bean to represent the Cafe side. For the other that is a vegan restaurant I decided to use a sprout as it help to suggest the idea of both Vegan as well as Organic ingredients used in the food items made at the cafe. So by combining these two points I created the final logo and the Logo itself represents the name of the Bean Sprout which makes it more easy to visually communicate the message and the name of the cafe removing any language barrier that might be there for tourists.

3. How did your sketches lead to this concept?

I started making the logo by using the things that we often see or use to represent a cafe like Kettles, Coffee, Cream, Bean, Cookies etc. Then after taking references and using a more common visual language I used a coffee bean and then used a budding sprout with it to represent the name of the cafe as well as vegan and organic ingredients used in the cafe which again is one of the common visual element used. Then by taking this concept I moved forward with different designs, typography and colors and ended up making this logo which in my view clearly describes my idea of the Cafe.
